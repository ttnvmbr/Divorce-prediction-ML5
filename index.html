Divorce prediction test

This test is based on a dataset that contains data about 150 couples with their corresponding Divorce Predictors Scale variables (DPS) on the basis of Gottman couples therapy. The couples are from various regions of Turkey wherein the records were acquired from face-to-face interviews from couples who were already divorced or happily married. The interview excisted of 54 questions. A decision tree revealed that only the answers of three questions are relevant to predict the outcome, based on the dataset.
